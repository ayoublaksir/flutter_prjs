# BeautyGlow App - Image Generation Prompts

This file contains detailed prompts for generating images for all beauty tips in the BeautyGlow app across 12 comprehensive batches covering skincare, makeup, haircare, and lifestyle.

## Image Style Guidelines

### General Photography Style
- **Lighting**: Natural, soft, and even lighting preferred
- **Composition**: Clean, minimalist backgrounds
- **Quality**: High-resolution, professional photography
- **Models**: Diverse representation across all images
- **Props**: Beauty products, tools, and natural elements

### Color Palettes by Category

**Skincare**: 
- Primary: Clean whites, soft blues, gentle pinks
- Accent: Natural greens, coral tones
- Mood: Fresh, clean, clinical yet warm

**Makeup**:
- Primary: Warm golds, rose golds, deep purples
- Accent: Classic reds, neutral browns
- Mood: Glamorous, artistic, sophisticated

**Haircare**:
- Primary: Warm browns, honey tones, natural blonde
- Accent: Soft silvers, deep chocolates
- Mood: Healthy, lustrous, natural

**Lifestyle**:
- Primary: Earthy greens, calming blues, warm neutrals
- Accent: Sunset oranges, lavender purples
- Mood: Serene, balanced, wellness-focused
